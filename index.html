Ayiti Start is a startup idea focused on helping Haitian businesses grow through simple digital tools and online support.

About

This startup is meant to support small businesses in Haiti by giving them a way to be seen online. The goal is to make it easier for local businesses to connect with more customers and share their products or services.

Services

Business Promotion

Help businesses create a stronger online presence.

Simple Website Tools

Offer basic website support for small businesses.

Customer Connection

Make it easier for people to learn about local businesses.

Why It Matters

Many small businesses have great ideas but may not have the resources to build a strong online presence. This startup could help fill that gap.
